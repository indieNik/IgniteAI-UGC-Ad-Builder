## Internal COGS Analysis

### Cost Breakdown (per 30-second video)

**Total COGS:** $5â€“$15 per video

**Primary Cost Drivers:**
1. **GPU Inference:** 90%+ of total COGS
   - Video generation models
   - Face animation/lip-sync
   - Background generation
2. **TTS APIs:** 5-10% of COGS
   - ElevenLabs
   - Azure TTS
3. **Storage & Bandwidth:** <5%

### Margin Implications
- Current API-based approach: ~30% gross margin
- Self-hosted models: Potential for >85% gross margin
- Critical to transition from "API-wrapping" to self-hosting
